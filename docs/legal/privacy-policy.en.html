Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-02

BonsaiLog ("the App") respects and protects your privacy. This policy describes how the App handles information.

1. Data Collected

The App stores the following information only on your device and does not transmit it to any server:

  • Bonsai records (name, species, style, acquired date, notes)
  • Care history (watering, pruning, fertilizing, etc.)
  • Photos (captured photos of your bonsai, stored in the app's document directory)
  • User preferences (language, theme, notification settings)

The App does not require account registration and does not collect personal information such as name, email, phone number, address, or precise location.

2. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob (Free plan only) — Banner ad delivery. Advertising IDs (IDFA / Android Advertising ID) are sent to AdMob. No ads are shown for Pro users. Google Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat — Subscription management. Uses anonymous IDs only; no personal information is transmitted. RevenueCat Privacy Policy
  • Apple App Store / Google Play — App distribution and billing.

3. Advertising and GDPR / IDFA

For users in the EEA, UK, or Switzerland, the App displays a GDPR-compliant consent form (UMP) at startup. On iOS, the App requests App Tracking Transparency permission before using IDFA. If you decline, you can still use all features of the App; non-personalized ads will be shown instead.

4. No AI / Recommendation Features

The App does not perform any AI-based species identification, disease diagnosis, or task recommendation. The App's role is to record (a journal), not to judge or recommend.

5. Where Data is Stored

All user data is stored locally on your device (SQLite / AsyncStorage / SecureStore / photo files). Uninstalling the App completely deletes all data. No cloud sync is performed.

6. Data Deletion Requests

Since the App does not send data to any server, a deletion request is completed by uninstalling the App. No separate contact is required.

7. Contact

For questions about this policy, please contact us via GitHub Issues (https://github.com/doooooraku/BonsaiLog/issues).

8. Changes to this Policy

This policy may change without prior notice. Updates will be communicated within the App or on this page.
